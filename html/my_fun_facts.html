Favorite Book

It Ends With Us By Colleen Hoover

It Ends With Us by Colleen Hoover is a romance novel based on the author's mother and father and their unhealthy relationship. I really enjoyed this book and had a hard time putting it down. It has an extremely powerful message about things women go through sometimes, and how your parents relationship can really effect your relationships as you grow up.
